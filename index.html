About

Cinematography is a craft science that discusses techniques for capturing images and at the same time combining these images so that they become a series of images that have the ability to convey ideas and stories. However, from some YouTube content creators in the field of Cinematography, they say that Cinematography is a process for making films/cinemas but only leads to pictures. So, it can be concluded that cinematography is the art of making films. The person in charge of making cinematography is usually called a cinematographer or Director of Photography.
